About the game
EvoWorld is a fast-paced, multiplayer survival game formerly known as flyordie where you start as a small fly and evolve into a variety of powerful creatures. Eat food to grow larger and stronger, and use your special abilities to hunt other players and avoid being hunted yourself.

As you evolve, you'll unlock new creatures with different abilities and playstyles. Some creatures are better at hunting, while others are better at defending themselves. You'll need to adapt your strategy to your chosen creature and the environment around you.

EvoWorld.io is a game of skill and strategy, but it's also a lot of fun. With its simple controls and addictive gameplay, EvoWorld.io is a game that anyone can enjoy.

Here are some of the things that make EvoWorld.io so much fun:
- A wide variety of creatures to evolve into, each with its own unique abilities.
- A fast-paced and challenging gameplay experience that requires both skill and strategy.
- A competitive multiplayer environment where you can test your skills against other players from around the world.
- A beautiful and vibrant world to explore, with a variety of different biomes to discover.

If you're looking for a fun and challenging multiplayer survival game, then EvoWorld.io is the perfect game for you.

On December 2020 the game name has been changed from FlyOrDie to EvoWorld.
Game Rules
To ensure a fair and enjoyable gaming experience for all players, please follow these rules:

- Do not share your account with other players. Account sharing is unfair to other players and is prohibited.

- It is forbidden to use scripts that give an unfair advantage in the game.

- Don't take advantage of game bugs to gain an advantage. Exploiting game bugs to gain an illegal advantage is strictly prohibited. Such behavior will result in permanent blocking of your account. If you found a serious error, please contact us by email.

- Do not attempt to cheat the payment system or fraudulently obtain in-game items or currency. Payment fraud is treated as a serious violation of the regulations.

- Respect other players and avoid toxic behavior. Do not use inappropriate nicknames.

Violation of the above rules may result in various consequences, depending on the severity of the violation and the history of the player's account.
Consequences may include temporary or permanent account suspension.

If you believe that you have been unfairly punished or have questions about the regulations, please contact our administration at contact@pixelvoices.com

Thank you for following these rules and we hope you enjoy the game!
